Post a specification

You can use the schools contract finder to help you find a supplier for you school to purchase goods or services.

Before you start

You need to write a ‘specification’ – the document you give to suppliers explaining what you want to buy.

Your specification should include:

  • a precise description of what you need
  • an explanation of what it should do, if needed
  • the amount you need
  • the quality it should be
  • when you need it
