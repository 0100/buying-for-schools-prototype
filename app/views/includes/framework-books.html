Books and materials

Based on your answers we think you should use the Eastern Shires Purchasing Organisation (ESPO) framework.

What it offers

The framework offers the supply of a range of adults and children’s books, e-books and e-audiobooks, including fiction, non-fiction and curriculum material, as well as educational software, CDs and DVDs.

Benefits

Key benefits of the framework are:

  • a range of suppliers to meet almost every need, from high street retail bookshops to specialist suppliers
  • discounts of over 41% are available off the price of some titles
  • pre-agreed terms and conditions to underpin all orders
