Recommended framework

Supply of natural gas and (daily/non-daily metered) and ancillary services

Based on your answers we think you should use the Crown Commercial Services (CCS) framework supply of natural gas and (daily/non-daily metered) and ancillary services.

What it offers

Single supplier agreement which will aggregate your volume and manage the excecution of trading on your behalf to deliver value for money in a complex, fast moving marketplace.

Includes liquefied petroleum gas, solid fuels including biomass, lubricants and grease, metering and telementary and fuels ancillary services.

Benefits

Key benefits of the framework are:

  • dedicated supplier-customer service team bypassing mainstream call centres
  • savings on administration costs
  • transparent, not for profit charging structure
