Electricity supply and ancillary services

Based on your answers we think you should use the Crown Commercial Services (CCS) framework electricity supply and ancillary services.

What it offers

The framework offers half-hourly metered electricity supply and ancillary services. It offers flexible short and long term procurement.offers 12 suppliers over 2 lots.

Includes liquefied petroleum gas, solid fuels including biomass, lubricants and grease, metering and telementary and fuels ancillary services.

Benefits

Key benefits of the framework are:

  • a dedicated customer service team
  • flexible short and long-term procurement
  • discounts in wholesale energy markets
