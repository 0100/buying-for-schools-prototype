ICT services for education

Based on your answers we think you should use the Crown Commercial Services (CCS) framework ICT services for education.

What it offers

The framework offers 20 suppliers in a single lot.

Provides a diverse range of services and solutions including:

  • supply and fit
  • support and maintenance
  • systems configuration and management
  • partial or fully managed ICT service
  • complete integrated service with other technologies/partners
  • supply and integration of solutions into both green field (new build) situations and legacy environments
  • ICT environments requiring integration and repurposing of existing ICT

Benefits

Key benefits of the framework are:

  • specialises in education settings
  • support from CCS with running a mini-competition
