Facilities management marketplace

Based on your answers we think you should use the Crown Commercial Services (CCS) framework facilities management marketplace.

What it offers

The framework offers 46 suppliers over 3 lots.

It includes:

  • contract management
  • contract mobilisation
  • maintenance services
  • horticultural services
  • statutory obligations
  • catering services
  • cleaning services
  • workplace FM services
  • reception services
  • security services
  • waste services
  • miscellaneous FM services
  • computer-aided facilities management (CAFM)
  • helpdesk services
  • management of billable works

Benefits

Key benefits of the framework are:

  • transparent, not for profit charging structure
  • regular updates on purchases and prices to support budgeting
  • encompasses lower value customer requirements
  • regional lot structure means you can procure services locally
