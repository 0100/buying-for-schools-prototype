Find a supplier for a school Version 2

Feature hypothesis

Hypothesis 1 – Find a supplier who is compliant and gives a good deal
Hypothesis 2 – Supported/managed procurement process

User need

I want help finding the best supplier who fits my needs, so that I procure at best value.

Method

The design will simplify how buyers find suppliers by pointing them to the correct procurement method and assisting them, by pointing them to a framework or helping them post a specificiation for tender.

Changes

Different buying methods are covered on a GOV.UK landing page, rather than having to ask the user about budget. The 'Find a framework' and 'Post a specification' flows are then treated as two seperate tools.
